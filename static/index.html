Our Story

More Than Just
a Cup of Coffee

Tucked in the heart of Indiranagar, Cozy Café was born from a simple dream — to create a place where every cup tells a story.

Founded in 2010 by two friends and coffee obsessives, our café started as a tiny espresso bar in a repurposed Victorian building. Word spread fast — people didn't just come for the coffee, they came for the feeling of belonging.

We source directly from small family farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Always fair trade. Always freshly roasted. Every drink crafted with intention.
